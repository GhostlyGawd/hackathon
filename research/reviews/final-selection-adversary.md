It did not accept any earlier ranking as authoritative. Build time is not a selection variable.

> **Final reconciliation status:** Focused audits of both [manufacturing ambiguity](manufacturing-ambiguity-novelty.md) and [package-intent resolution](package-intent-novelty.md) overturned the successive provisional leads. **No current mechanism deserves unconditional product selection.** Capability settlement is the sole recommended next falsification candidate because it has the strongest causal evidence and lowest authority burden; it becomes the build choice only if the decisive experiment at the end passes.

## Verdict

The broad local ADE / causal-history direction does **not** survive as the hackathon thesis. Its attractive parts are mostly an assembly of established ideas, and its remaining wedge has an unresolved oracle paradox. It may eventually supply one feature to a larger developer environment, but it is not the strongest category or first-place bet in this tournament.

The surviving slate contains one validation priority and four conditional or research wildcards:

1. **Validation priority: capability settlement** — keep an AI-assisted artifact complete while leaving the capability claim provisional until delayed, independent near- and far-transfer performance settles it.
2. **Conditional human-impact wildcard: worker-governed private cohort pay reconciliation** — detect repeated pay-event transformations across consenting workers without pooling raw wages, then let the workers decide whether a trusted advocate receives the cited evidence.
3. **Developer-tools research wildcard: authority-continuity dependency receipt** — when a trust anchor exists before candidate selection, preserve a verified chain from that authority through source and publisher to an immutable artifact and bounded compatibility witness.
4. **Technical research wildcard: counterfactual MCP tool admission** — treat compatibility as a versioned property of a model/client/tool composition by testing representative tasks with and without a candidate server against external state assertions.

**Manufacturing research wildcard:** **function-grounded ambiguity witness** — given a responsible engineer's predeclared critical-to-quality (CTQ) predicate, search for two released-package-conforming parts with opposite CTQ outcomes. It is not a finalist until it beats checker-plus-tolerance-analysis baselines on real cases.

**Decisive recommendation:** do not yet select a product. Run the capability-settlement falsifier as the only promotion test. If it fails, reopen mechanism generation rather than automatically falling back to one of the feature-shaped wildcards.

## Why none is unconditional

Capability settlement has the strongest direct causal problem evidence and safest authority boundary, but delayed transfer and mastery assessment are familiar; the unproven category claim is a portable provisional/settled capability object across assistance tools. Private cohort reconciliation attacks a genuine evidence and power asymmetry, but WageWatch already supports coworker collective claims and the deployed Shipt Calculator already aggregated worker pay data with nonprofit worker groups; privacy-thresholded transformation matching is a residual feature, not clean white space. The package audit found capability resolution, natural-language package selection, provenance, risk gates, and pre-install enforcement already covered; only an authority-continuity receipt survives, and it may be an evidence-rich allowlist. Manufacturing imports its materiality oracle from an engineer-owned CTQ and then overlaps tolerance analysis. MCP admission remains technically clean but below the tournament's evidence gate.

Several attractive mechanisms do not enter the five:

- **Binding transfer-credit offers** solve the authority problem honestly, but the receiving registrar's commitment does most of the causal work. Without participating institutions, the product is a proposal generator and the demo is theater.
- **Appeal-derived denial regression gates** have exceptional model leverage and impact, but require payer- or regulator-side data and enforcement authority. A patient-side prototype cannot impose the gate it claims changes outcomes.
- **Factorial merge cohorts** are externally testable, but remain combinatorial CI plus a joint-ownership object. Merge queues, feature-interaction testing, and CooperBench make the residual category claim too thin.
- **Revocable merge leases** are socially interesting, but progressive delivery and guarded rollback already supply most of the causal loop, and many open-source changes have no safe canary population.
- **Tenant contestability windows** change the right state at the right time, but landlord and consumer-reporting-agency covenants, not GPT-5.6, create the remedy.

### Scoring attacks

**Function-grounded manufacturing witness.** The 4 in Technological Implementation is available only if GPT-5.6 performs constrained cross-artifact mapping while CAD and tolerance tools independently prove both source conformance and opposite outcomes on a predeclared CTQ. A drawing-markup chatbot earns a 1 or 2. Design and idea quality fall to 3 after the focused audit: without a separately authoritative CTQ, two conforming parts demonstrate permitted variation rather than a defect; once that CTQ is supplied, F4-, Autodesk-, and CETOL-style deterministic tooling already performs much of the causal analysis. The paired minimal witness may improve review, but that is an unproven interaction advantage, not yet a standalone category.

**Authority-continuity dependency receipt.** The [focused audit](package-intent-novelty.md) kills “dependencies addressed by intent” as a new primitive: Debian/RPM/Gradle-style capability resolution is old; PySelect and related systems already map natural-language needs to packages; Snyk, Socket, and Phylum already place health or policy gates in agent/package install paths; and provenance standards already bind source, publisher, build, and artifact. The only residue requires a trusted authority edge that predates candidate discovery. GPT-5.6 may make the receipt cheaper to author, but deterministic policy owns admission. If no independent authority exists, the system can recommend but cannot autonomously authorize. That bootstrap dilemma lowers both implementation and idea scores.

**Private cohort reconciliation.** Potential impact remains high because the mechanism can turn isolated, deniable discrepancies into worker-controlled corroboration without declaring illegality. Novelty falls after the live substitute check: [WageWatch](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wagewatchapp.wagewatch) already invites coworkers to explore group claims, and the peer-reviewed [Shipt Calculator deployment](https://doi.org/10.1145/3570601) aggregated pay data from 140 workers with nonprofit worker groups to expose an unannounced pay change. The residual is privacy-preserving comparison of transformation patterns and worker-controlled threshold release. Implementation remains 3 unless privacy is technically real—local extraction, secure aggregation or an audited equivalent, minimum-cell rules, deletion, and consented release. It cannot lead without worker-organization governance and evidence that this residual changes correction outcomes.

**Capability settlement.** This has the strongest direct causal evidence: assisted task performance and later unassisted performance can diverge. It also has an excellent, safe demo state: `task complete / capability provisional` changes only after an independently scored transfer action. It loses idea-quality points because delayed assessment, mastery states, adaptive probes, and independent next-item measurement already exist. Khanmigo now measures next-item correctness without the tutor; LMS mastery systems, AI vivas, and practical assessment are close substitutes. A portable settlement object plus far transfer is a meaningful refinement, but until educators value that portability it is not category-shaping.

**Counterfactual MCP admission.** This is technically excellent and genuinely relational: Inspector, conformance, registry review, Microsoft MCP Interviewer, and tool search evaluate or select tools but do not publicly establish the marginal effect of server B on a pinned composition and task class. Yet paired trials, A/B testing, and delta debugging are established methods, while Braintrust and LangSmith can host the experiment. More seriously, the underlying pain still has only one young research line and may shrink as model-side tool search improves. Equal judging weights prevent a 4 in implementation from hiding a 2 in impact.

## Wildcard after focused audit: function-grounded manufacturing witness

**Actor and event:** A manufacturing engineer is about to release a part package to a machinist or supplier. The drawing, 3D model, and PMI appear individually acceptable, but the engineer's signed assembly or quality requirement may not be controlled by the package.

**Mechanism:** A responsible engineer or requirements system first supplies an authoritative critical-to-quality predicate such as clearance, alignment, insertion, sealing, or inspection access. GPT-5.6 uses original-detail multimodal input and long-context reasoning to propose semantically distinct source interpretations and compile them into typed constraints. Deterministic CAD and tolerance tools search for a minimal pair that both satisfy the released package but produce opposite outcomes on that CTQ. GPT-5.6 cannot infer materiality, certify conformance, or authorize release. A responsible engineer resolves the missing constraint, and the signed clarification becomes part of the release package.

> Because the system constructs and independently validates **two released-package-conforming parts with opposite outcomes on an engineer-declared CTQ**, the machinist no longer has to argue from intuition that the package undercontrols that requirement, which changes downstream guesswork into a pre-cut engineering decision, measurable by engineer-confirmed consequential ambiguities at a fixed false-block rate and time to signed clarification.

### Three-minute causal spine

1. Show a real NIST-style CAD/PMI assembly package plus one signed CTQ that a conventional completeness or standards check accepts.
2. Run the ambiguity search. GPT-5.6 proposes two constrained interpretations; the interface exposes the exact drawing/model spans behind each assumption.
3. Build both actual models. A deterministic validator certifies that both satisfy the released part constraints.
4. Evaluate the predeclared CTQ with the same deterministic assembly/tolerance model. One variant passes and the other fails.
5. The engineer chooses or edits one interpretation. The release state visibly changes from `apparently releasable` to `ambiguity witnessed` to `signed intent`; the losing interpretation becomes a regression case.
6. Show the boundary: when a second solver-valid interpretation cannot be produced, the product says `NO WITNESS FOUND`, not “unambiguous.”

This remains demonstrably better than prose or an activity trace, but visual force is not proof of incremental product value. The strongest baseline receives the same CTQ and runs conventional tolerance analysis.

### Closest substitutes and residual white space

- [Siemens PMI Advisor](https://www.plm.automation.siemens.com/media/global/de/Pr%C3%A4sentation%20PDF-%20Model%20Based%20Definition%20-%20Komplexit%C3%A4t%20in%20der%20Konstruktion%20beherrschen_tcm53-103590.pdf) and [NIST's conformance work](https://www.nist.gov/publications/guide-nist-pmi-cad-models-and-cad-system-pmi-modeling-capability-verification-testing) validate whether PMI conforms to standards and whether CAD systems preserve it.
- [Autodesk Inventor Tolerance Analysis](https://www.autodesk.com/products/inventor-tolerance-analysis/overview) computes stackups and fit for a selected interpretation.
- [F4](https://f4.dev/) interprets GD&T, validates syntax and datum structures, visualizes tolerance zones, and performs stack-ups without an LLM.
- [Sigmetrix CETOL 6σ](https://www.sigmetrix.com/software/CETOL) predicts how dimensional and assembly variation affects product performance inside major CAD systems.
- Current AI drawing-review products such as [CoLab AutoReview](https://www.colabsoftware.com/post/best-ai-tools-for-manufacturing-drawing-checks), [Leo AI](https://www.getleo.ai/blog/ai-engineering-drawing-review), and [SOURCIX](https://sourcixai.com/ai-tools/drawing-verifier/) advertise missing-dimension, standards, manufacturability, and tolerance-conflict findings.
- A [2026 research system](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.18296) already maps 2D annotations to 3D features and escalates unresolved ambiguity to a human.

Those substitutes sharply narrow the claim. The proposed product is not “AI checks drawings.” Its residual capability is **function-grounded constructive underdetermination**: for a predeclared CTQ, find the smallest source-conforming pair with opposite outcomes and show the missing constraint that eliminates the bad branch. A focused scan found no public primary source claiming that exact interaction, but the mechanism is readily absorbable by CAD, MBD-validation, and tolerance-analysis incumbents.

### Decisive falsifier and confidence

Create a benchmark of clean packages, engineer-seeded cases, and historical drawing/model ambiguities. Give every system the same authoritative CTQ. Compare a standards/completeness checker, checker plus conventional tolerance analysis, a GPT-5.6 redline reviewer, and the paired witness. The primary endpoint is engineer-confirmed consequential ambiguities found at a fixed false-release-block rate; also test whether the pair reduces time-to-disposition versus a precise textual warning.

Kill the idea if:

- its “two interpretations” are merely ordinary tolerance extremes already exposed by stackup analysis;
- almost every successful case requires the CTQ to state the missing requirement so explicitly that conventional tolerance analysis finds the same defect;
- either candidate violates a source constraint when checked independently;
- engineers call the witnesses artificial or non-manufacturable;
- engineers resolve a direct warning faster than the paired artifact;
- GPT-5.6 ablation does not remove a meaningful class of true positives;
- the model cannot reliably connect 2D annotations, 3D features, assembly context, and process notes;
- current products already generate the same constructive witness.

**Confidence: medium that the interaction is distinct; high that it is only a wildcard/feature today.** The authoritative CTQ fixes the truth problem but compresses the novelty into the gap left by deterministic tolerance analysis.

## Dependency wildcard after focused audit: authority-continuity receipt

**Actor and event:** A coding agent decides it needs a dependency and emits a plausible package name. An attacker can register that previously nonexistent name, turning the agent's old hallucination into an apparently resolvable install.

**Mechanism:** The task, current repository, organization policy, or explicit human must establish a trusted source root before candidate selection. GPT-5.6 can recover the bounded required API and propose a compatibility witness, while deterministic tools verify continuity from the preexisting authority through source, publisher, provenance, artifact digest, and executed behavior. Candidate-controlled claims can corroborate but cannot create authority. Open-ended discovery remains recommendation-only; ambiguity produces `IDENTITY UNRESOLVED`.

The closest substitutes now include npm [provenance](https://docs.npmjs.com/generating-provenance-statements/) and [trusted publishing](https://docs.npmjs.com/trusted-publishers/), SLSA expectations, capability-based package resolution, PySelect-style natural-language package selection, and current Snyk, Socket, and Phylum pre-install gates. The exact residual is authority continuity, not semantic resolution, package recommendation, provenance, risk scoring, or installation enforcement.

The decisive benchmark must compare registry-existence checks, provenance-only checks, current pre-install gates, a human-authored source allowlist plus deterministic verification, and authority-continuity receipts with and without GPT-5.6. Use temporally sealed slopsquat decoys with valid attacker-controlled provenance, multiple honest providers, and rare legitimate packages. Kill it if any candidate bootstraps its own authority, if source policy matches admission outcomes without materially more human work, or if legitimate discovery collapses into per-package manual approval.

**Confidence: medium that the invariant is useful; medium-high that it is only a package-manager/harness feature today.** GPT-5.6's only defensible contribution is reducing contract and witness-authoring work, which remains unproven.

## Why the local ADE / causal-history idea fails

The user's suspicion was correct: the broad direction risks becoming planning and verification theater wrapped in a local environment.

### The category shell is not a mechanism

“A revolutionary local ADE for vibe coding” names a surface and deployment preference. Local hosting, agent orchestration, repository memory, previews, plans, explanations, and provenance do not by themselves change the causal failure. Current code hosts, IDEs, architecture-governance products, local agents, sandbox products, and repository-intelligence tools already supply those features in combinations.

### Nearly every causal-history component has prior art

- ROSE, Microsoft Rex, CodeScene, CKB, and related systems mine co-change and warn about missing companion edits.
- Software Change Contracts already describe executable relations across versions; the term and concept are not new.
- Delta debugging, mutation testing, and counterexample-guided invariant mining already use intervention or falsification to qualify behavioral claims.
- GitHub/GitLab queues test combined changes; CoAgent/MTPO already supplies advisory transaction control and selective repair for concurrent agents.
- Mneme and adjacent repository-memory products preserve decisions and provenance.

The only defensible residue is narrow: promote a history-mined companion-change hypothesis only after a hermetic `full change passes -> semantic ablation fails -> restoration rescues` witness, then preserve the minimal replayable witness with provenance, scope, and expiry.

### The oracle paradox is fatal to the broad product claim

If the oracle that exposes the ablation already runs on every pull request, the new guard duplicates CI and mainly adds explanation or speed. If the oracle was authored by the same patch or inferred by the same model, the proof is circular. If the decisive observation came from production, an external service, unavailable data, or a flaky historical environment, there may be no durable guard to compile. If the witness is frozen indefinitely, it turns accidental old behavior into policy and rejects valid alternative implementations.

Historical replay also selects for easy, hermetic pure-code failures while excluding the distributed state, migrations, external APIs, and data drift that make the real failures valuable. This is not a small implementation inconvenience; it undercuts the causal and market claim.

### Rubric consequence

A polished ADE could score well on implementation, but the central idea would score only 1–2 after the prior-art scan, and its impact demo would usually reduce to “CI now fails when I remove a required change.” That is sophisticated regression testing, not a new outcome for the developer. Calling it causal version control or a new development paradigm would overstate the evidence.

The narrow historical-witness experiment should survive only as research. Advance it later if a blinded, time-split evaluation shows incremental real omission defects caught beyond unchanged CI, materially better interruption precision than Rex-style co-change warnings, reproducible independent oracles, expiry, and acceptance of valid alternative implementations. Even then, the honest product is a **historical behavioral witness compiler**, not a revolutionary ADE.

## Final decision rule

Do not choose dependency admission because it sounds secure, manufacturing because it looks cinematic, worker evidence because the harm is severe, capability settlement because it has the cleanest study, or an ADE because it sounds ambitious. Each current mechanism retains a category-level failure: allowlist collapse, tolerance-analysis collapse, collective-audit prior art plus safety, ordinary mastery-assessment collapse, or the ADE oracle paradox.

On the evidence currently available, **none deserves unconditional selection**. The decisive recommendation is to give **capability settlement one promotion experiment**, not to declare it the product. It has the best causal evidence, an external outcome, low institutional authority, and a complete three-minute state change. It promotes only if a portable delayed-settlement object and GPT-5.6-generated far-transfer probes add measured value beyond an ordinary mastery system and teacher-authored item bank. If it fails, kill the current slate and restart mechanism generation rather than inheriting the next-highest score. The ADE does not survive.

## Reconciliation addendum — focused manufacturing audit

**Added 2026-07-19 after reading the focused [manufacturing ambiguity novelty audit](manufacturing-ambiguity-novelty.md). This addendum supersedes the initial manufacturing-first recommendation and every earlier pairwise row that favored it.**

The original selection implicitly treated “different assembly/process consequences” as a materiality oracle without requiring that oracle to be independently authoritative. That was not rigorous enough. If a released package defines constraint set \(S\), many different parts in \(F(S)\) are intentionally conforming. Producing \(x,y \in F(S)\) with \(x \neq y\) proves variation, not a defect. A consequential witness additionally needs a predeclared function or CTQ predicate \(Q\) for which \(Q(x) \neq Q(y)\).

That correction creates the decisive compression:

1. Without authoritative \(Q\), GPT-5.6 can invent visually dramatic but irrelevant “materiality” and cannot safely block release.
2. With authoritative \(Q\), existing tolerance-analysis and assembly-variation systems already perform much of the causal work.
3. The residual paired witness may still improve discovery and engineer review across ambiguous 2D/3D mappings, but no evidence yet shows it adds real-case recall or reduces disposition time beyond checker-plus-tolerance analysis.

The function-grounded CTQ reshape is therefore scientifically coherent but **cannot lead now**. It is a wildcard experiment and, on current evidence, more likely a plugin or feature for CAD/PLM/tolerance-analysis systems than a standalone first-place thesis. It re-enters finalist consideration only if blinded historical evaluation shows materially higher engineer-confirmed recall at a fixed false-block rate, a faster disposition than a precise warning, and a meaningful GPT-5.6 ablation effect.

Interim selection at that stage, subsequently superseded by the package audit below:

1. **Interim lead:** intent-bound dependency resolution.
2. **Runner-up, conditional:** worker-governed private cohort pay reconciliation.
3. **Lower-risk fallback:** capability settlement.
4. **Technical wildcard among active finalists:** counterfactual MCP tool admission.
5. **Research wildcard outside the finalist order:** function-grounded manufacturing ambiguity witness.

This update reinforces the larger selection rule: an impressive counterexample is not enough; the consequence must come from an independent oracle, and the resulting loop must outperform the strongest deterministic substitute.

### Final reconciliation — focused package audit

**Added 2026-07-19 after reading the focused [package-intent novelty audit](package-intent-novelty.md). This section supersedes the dependency-first revision above.**

The package audit found an authority-bootstrap dilemma symmetric with manufacturing's materiality problem:

1. If the resolver derives authority from the candidate's registry record, repository, documentation, provenance, or shallow API behavior, an attacker can manufacture a complete and internally consistent false identity.
2. If an independent trust root must predate candidate discovery, open-ended package discovery cannot autonomously authorize its result. The mechanism becomes an evidence-rich source allowlist or SLSA expectation gate unless it saves substantial human work.
3. Capability-based resolution, natural-language recommendation, package knowledge graphs, provenance, package identity standards, health scoring, and pre-install enforcement already have current research or shipping prior art. “Dependencies addressed by intent” is not a defensible novelty claim.

The narrow surviving artifact is an **authority-continuity dependency-introduction receipt**. It may be valuable infrastructure, but current evidence does not justify a first-place product thesis, and GPT-5.6 is structural only if it materially reduces contract/witness authoring at equal admission accuracy to deterministic source policy.

The remaining alternatives also do not earn an unconditional promotion:

- **Worker-private cohort reconciliation:** real harm and potential power shift, but WageWatch already supports coworker group claims, the Shipt Calculator already deployed aggregate worker pay auditing with worker organizations, the model role is secondary, and privacy/retaliation require actual worker governance.
- **Capability settlement:** strongest causal evidence and safest validation, but it risks being delayed assessment plus an ordinary mastery state. Portability across assistance tools and validated far transfer are hypotheses.
- **Function-grounded manufacturing witness:** coherent only with an external CTQ; once supplied, incumbent tolerance analysis performs much of the causal work.
- **Authority-continuity dependency receipt:** coherent only with an external trust root; once supplied, source allowlists and existing provenance/policy gates perform much of the causal work.

#### Decisive recommendation and falsifier

Run one blinded capability-settlement study in a bounded adult-learning domain with deterministic or independently scored outcomes:

1. Compare ordinary LMS/mastery state plus a teacher-authored delayed item bank against the portable `task complete / capability provisional / capability settled` protocol.
2. Cross GPT-5.6-generated near/far-transfer probes with teacher-authored probes and a smaller-model generator; score all outcomes independently.
3. Measure delayed near transfer, far transfer, retention, confidence calibration, learner burden, educator review time, accommodations, and whether learners change practice decisions.
4. Ask learners and educators whether preserving the provisional/settled object across different AI assistants changes a consequential decision, rather than merely adding another quiz or grade.

**Promote capability settlement only if** the portable state plus GPT-5.6 probe generation improves independently measured transfer/calibration or reduces expert authoring burden at equal validity, without surveillance, punitive use, or materially higher learner burden.

**Kill it—and reopen the idea search—if** a teacher-authored item bank and ordinary mastery state perform equivalently; users experience settlement as a redundant second grade; valid far-transfer probes require prohibitive expert work; GPT-5.6 does not outperform simpler generation at equal validity; or accommodations make the state misleading. Do not automatically promote worker pay, manufacturing, or dependency receipts after that failure; each has its own unresolved causal gate.

This is the final selection: **no unconditional winner; capability settlement receives the only promotion test.**
