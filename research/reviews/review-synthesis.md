# Independent-review synthesis

Research snapshot: 2026-07-19. This synthesis was written after the market and validity/safety reviewers completed their work, but before any mechanism reviewer returned. It preserves disagreement rather than averaging ranks into false precision.

## What each review was allowed to answer

- The **market skeptic** asked whether the pain remains under-served after named substitutes, regulation, and incumbent movement.
- The **validity/safety critic** asked whether the exact failure claim is supported, safely bounded, and evaluable with independent ground truth.
- The **judge lens** did not rank problems. It established that a finalist needs a real problem, a distinct mechanism, non-trivial working code, and a coherent product that proves a changed outcome quickly.

Consequently, a high rank in one review cannot cancel a fatal flaw in another.

## Consensus survivors

Only four surfaces appear in both independent top tens:

1. maintaining OSS openness while bounding trust cost;
2. recovering undocumented companion-change obligations;
3. giving hourly workers an auditable pay calculation despite record asymmetry;
4. making tenant-screening errors contestable before the opportunity disappears.

Transfer credit also appears in both lists, but the market review ranks it ninth specifically because independent software cannot make the result binding. It survives as a falsification test for mechanisms that claim to create certainty without authority.

## Product-space versus evidence-space disagreement

The central disagreement is informative:

- **Education and agent oversight have the strongest direct causal or practitioner evidence.** They are also surrounded by tutors, learning modes, oral verification, process capture, code review, tests, and agent-eval products.
- **MCP composition and cumulative repository interactions have the cleanest novelty.** Their direct harm, stable prevalence, and willingness-to-pay evidence are much weaker and may shift as models and clients improve.
- **Wage and tenant evidence failures combine real harm with weaker substitutes.** They also expose the builder to authority, privacy, retaliation, legal-boundary, and affected-user-validation constraints that a polished document demo would not resolve.

The mechanism round therefore has two different jobs:

1. find a new feedback loop for a well-proven problem that does not reproduce an incumbent category; or
2. show that an emerging devtool problem produces a stable, measurable failure worth solving rather than merely an impressive technical demo.

## Advancement gates

No mechanism advances unless it answers the relevant gate:

### Maintainer trust

- What evidence cannot be cheaply fabricated by the same agent that produced the contribution?
- Does maintainer time fall without suppressing valuable newcomers or shifting equal work onto them?
- Is the outcome contribution quality/follow-through rather than an AI-authorship or style score?

### Historical and cumulative code interactions

- What concrete failure is predicted before existing CI or a merge queue finds it?
- How is causal obligation separated from historical coincidence or obsolete practice?
- Can success be evaluated time-split on repositories the model did not train the rule against?

### MCP composition

- Does adding an individually valid tool reproducibly reduce task success across pinned model/client/task configurations?
- Can the mechanism restore performance without manually authored golden answers or merely hiding most tools?
- Does a user currently spend material time or accept material risk because this signal is absent?

### Agent oversight

- Does the mechanism improve correct accept/reject decisions on independently seeded defects, not just reviewer confidence?
- Does it reduce total review time and workload relative to diff plus tests?
- Is its evidence independent of the originating model?

### Learning

- Is success delayed, unassisted near/far transfer rather than assisted task completion?
- Is the intervention structurally different from a tutor that withholds answers or an AI viva?
- Does it avoid authorship inference, hidden telemetry, punishment, and inaccessible one-size-fits-all assessment?

### Pay and tenant screening

- What authoritative fact becomes available that the affected person did not already control?
- What outcome changes before retaliation or the housing opportunity disappears?
- Who validates legal/rule accuracy, identity matching, privacy, and consequential false allegations?

### Transfer credit

- What makes the answer binding, or how does the product visibly avoid false certainty?
- Can a receiving institution attest to requirement-level outcomes before commitment?
- Does the mechanism change institutional timing rather than merely improve an unofficial estimate?

## Decision rule for the mechanism round

The tournament will not select the highest-scoring topic. It will select a causal loop that survives its surface-specific gate, remains meaningfully different from the strongest named substitute, exposes uncertainty, and produces an externally verifiable state change in one coherent demonstration.
